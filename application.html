Probationary Period and Formal Employment

The probationary period is five (5) working days, during which you will receive relevant training and undergo a work task assessment. Upon passing the probationary period assessment, you will become a formal employee of Publicis Groupe. As a formal employee, you will be entitled to the following company benefits:

  • Health insurance
  • 401(k) retirement plan
  • Paid time off
  • Employee training and development opportunities
  • Remote work equipment subsidy (depending on the position)
